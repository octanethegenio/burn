BURN: FIRST LAUNCH ON macOS

Burn is not yet notarized by Apple, so macOS will block the first launch.

1. Move Burn.app to Applications and double-click it.
2. On the “Burn” Not Opened warning, click Done — not Move to Trash.
3. Open System Settings > Privacy & Security, scroll down, and click Open Anyway beside Burn.
4. Confirm Open. Burn will open in your default browser.

This approval is normally required only once.

Only override this warning if you downloaded Burn from:
https://github.com/octanethegenio/burn/releases
